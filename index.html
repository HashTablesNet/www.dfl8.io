Deflationary Wrapped Eth

dFL8 is an ERC20 token similar to Wrapped Ether, with a 1% burn to counter Ethereum's native inflation.

Find Out More

Deflation is here to stay!

In 2019 Bomb released their deflationary token that took the crypto world by storm. Instead of idly watching the experiment run its course, the community doubled down and created an entire ecosystem around the idea. As such, DFL8 seeks to fill the void by becoming the standard version of wrapped ETH used to interact with other deflationary tokens, or simply hodl ETH without worrying about inflation.

Deflationary

We combat Ethereum's inflation by:
- 1% Transactional burn
- 1% Wrap burn
- 1% Unwrap Burn

Wrapped

DFL8 tokens are minted when you lock Ether in the contract, based on the current value. The value is based on the number of ETH to DFL8 in the contract. You can unwrap your DFL8 for ETH at any time.

Security

The contract owner has limited ability to whitelist exchange addresses to be exempt from burn, and to change the name of the token in the future. No tokens can be minted without depositing ETH. The contract has been audited and is open source.

Risk

This token aims to be low risk and truely deflationary, meaning the value of your tokens will never go down. Balances can be reduced by wrapping, unwrapping, and transfering the tokens only. If you wrap 1 ETH and immediately unwrap, you will receive 0.99ETH back as the maximum possible loss.
